Quadratic Equation Calculator

In algebra, a quadratic equation (from the Latin quadratus for "square") is any equation that can be rearranged into the standard form: ax2 + bx + c = 0, where x represents an unknown variable, and a, b, and c represent known numbers, with a ≠ 0. The solutions to a quadratic equation can be found using the quadratic formula: x = (-b ± √(b2 - 4ac)) / 2a.
